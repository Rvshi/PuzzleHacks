About

PuzzleHacks is a campus-wide team-based puzzle scavenger hunt at Duke. Puzzles will be hidden throughout campus, involving topics from multiple disciplines, including computer science to biology to history. The answers to one puzzle will be hints to the next puzzle. Teams must include people from diverse backgrounds in order to be successful. Good luck!
